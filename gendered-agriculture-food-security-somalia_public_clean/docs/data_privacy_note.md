# Data privacy and responsible use

This public mini-project does not share raw SIHBS household microdata.

Only aggregate, non-identifiable outputs are included. These outputs are designed for public communication, portfolio presentation and figure reproduction. No household-level records, personal identifiers, geocoordinates, restricted survey files, internal audit datasets, or draft manuscripts are included.

Researchers who wish to reproduce the full analysis should access the original survey data through the official data provider and follow the applicable data-use conditions.

Do not upload raw survey files or household-level extracts to this repository.
